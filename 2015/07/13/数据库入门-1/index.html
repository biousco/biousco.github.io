  • 选择select（$\sigma$）

$$\sigma_{p} (r) = \left \{ t|t\in r\; and \; p(t) \right \}$$

$$\sigma_{deptname=”Physics”}(instructor)$$

$$\prod_{A1,A2,A3…,Ak}(r)$$
